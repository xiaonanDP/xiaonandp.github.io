Intent Capture / 要干嘛

Remember why you picked up your phone—before the feed steals your focus.

Capture a one-line intent in seconds. It stays on your Lock Screen, Home Screen widget, and Live Activity until you finish or dismiss it—no account, all local on device.

English

Intent Capture freezes the thought you had when you unlocked your phone. Not a todo app—one short line, one active intent. Your data stays on your device.

Core Features

  • Three-second capture - Open the app, type one line, save—no projects or due dates
  • Lock Screen widget - See your intent the moment you wake your phone
  • Home Screen widget - Mark done or dismiss without opening the app
  • Live Activity - Gentle ambient reminder while an intent is active
  • Control Center - Quick capture from Control Center
  • Shortcuts - Automate capture with Siri and Shortcuts
  • Local history - Lightweight past-intent history stored on device
  • No account - Core features work without sign-in

How to Set Up Widgets

  • Lock Screen - Long-press the Lock Screen → Customize → tap the widget area below the clock → search for Intent Capture → add the widget → Done
  • Home Screen - Long-press the Home Screen → tap + → search for Intent Capture → choose a size → Add Widget
  • Control Center - Open Settings → Control Center → add Intent Capture if available on your iOS version

How to Use

  • Tap Capture Intent in the app (or use Control Center / Shortcuts) and enter why you unlocked
  • Your intent appears on Lock Screen, Home Screen widget, and Live Activity
  • Tap Done when finished, or Dismiss if you no longer need the reminder
  • Only one intent is active at a time—capturing a new one auto-dismisses the previous active intent

FAQ

  • Widget not updating? iOS may delay widget refreshes. Open the app once, or remove and re-add the widget. Ensure Intent Capture is allowed background refresh in Settings if needed.
  • Live Activity not showing? Live Activities require a supported device and an active intent. Check Settings → Intent Capture → Live Activities is enabled.
  • Can I use it offline? Yes. Capture, widgets, and history work without an internet connection.
  • Where is my data stored? On your device only. Intents are saved locally and shared with the widget extension via App Group storage—not uploaded to our servers.
  • How do I delete history? Swipe or delete past intents from the in-app history screen.
中文（简体）

「要干嘛」帮你在信息流抢走注意力之前，记住刚刚解锁手机时那个一闪而过的念头。 不是待办清单——一行字，一个当前意图，数据只保存在本机。

核心功能

  • 三秒捕捉 - 打开应用，写一行字，保存——无需项目或截止日期
  • 锁屏小组件 - 拿起手机第一眼就能看到当前意图
  • 主屏幕小组件 - 无需打开应用即可标记完成或忽略
  • 实时活动 - 意图活跃时安静陪伴，不吵闹但始终可见
  • 控制中心 - 从控制中心快速录入
  • 快捷指令 - 配合 Siri 与快捷指令一键捕捉
  • 本地历史 - 轻量历史记录，仅保存在设备上
  • 无需账号 - 核心功能不强制登录注册

如何设置小组件

  • 锁屏 - 长按锁屏 → 自定义 → 点击时钟下方小组件区域 → 搜索「要干嘛」或 Intent Capture → 添加 → 完成
  • 主屏幕 - 长按主屏幕 → 点左上角 + → 搜索 Intent Capture → 选择尺寸 → 添加小组件
  • 控制中心 - 打开「设置 → 控制中心」，在支持的系统版本中添加 Intent Capture

如何使用

  • 在应用中点击「记住」（或通过控制中心 / 快捷指令），写下你解锁的原因
  • 意图会出现在锁屏、主屏幕小组件与实时活动中
  • 完成后点「完成」，不再需要时点「忽略」
  • 同一时间仅有一个活跃意图——捕捉新意图会自动忽略之前的活跃意图

常见问题

  • 小组件不更新？ 系统可能延迟刷新小组件。可打开应用一次，或移除后重新添加小组件。
  • 实时活动不显示？ 需要支持的设备且有活跃意图。请检查「设置 → Intent Capture → 实时活动」是否已开启。
  • 可以离线使用吗？ 可以。捕捉、小组件与历史记录均无需联网。
  • 数据保存在哪里？ 仅保存在您的设备上，通过 App Group 与小组件扩展共享，不会上传至我们的服务器。
  • 如何删除历史？ 在应用内的历史记录中删除过往意图即可。
