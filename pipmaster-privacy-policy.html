Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

PiPmaster (画中画大师)

This Privacy Policy explains how PiPmaster (the "Application") handles your information. PiPmaster is provided by TwoStones (the "Service Provider") as a commercial app.

本隐私政策用于说明 PiPmaster（画中画大师，以下简称“本应用”）如何处理您的信息。 本应用由 TwoStones（以下简称“服务提供方”）提供。

Information We Collect / 我们收集的信息

PiPmaster is designed with a local-first approach. Core media and script content are handled on device. We do not require account registration to use core features.

PiPmaster 采用本地优先设计。核心媒体和提词内容在设备本地处理。 使用核心功能无需注册账号。

Permissions Used / 权限说明

  • Camera / 相机: used only when you enable camera PiP features.
  • Microphone / 麦克风: used for recording scenarios and speech-follow teleprompter mode.
  • Photos / 相册: used when you import images or videos, or save/export media.
  • Speech Recognition / 语音识别: used only for optional speech-follow teleprompter features.

Permissions are requested only when needed for a specific feature. 仅在对应功能需要时申请相关权限。

How We Use Information / 信息用途

Information and permissions are used only to provide app functions such as picture-in-picture camera, teleprompter guidance, media import/export, and local session processing.

相关信息仅用于提供本应用功能，例如画中画相机、提词跟读、媒体导入导出和本地会话处理。

Data Sharing / 数据共享

We do not sell your personal data. Media and content remain on your device unless you choose to export or share.

我们不会出售您的个人数据。除非您主动导出或分享，媒体与内容默认保留在您的设备上。

Children's Privacy / 儿童隐私

The Application is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童。如您认为儿童提供了个人信息，请联系我们以便采取处理措施。

Security / 安全

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information processed by the Application.

我们采取合理的技术与组织措施，保护本应用处理的信息安全。
