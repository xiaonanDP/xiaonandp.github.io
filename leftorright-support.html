Would You Rather: Left or Right / 二选一：大冒险

Tilt your head, pick a side, record the chaos.

Turn every dilemma into a shareable moment—pick a theme, tilt left or right on camera to choose, and finish with a funny result summary plus a vertical video ready to save or share.

English

Would You Rather: Left or Right is a party-style challenge game. Two options appear beside your front-camera preview; tilt your head left or right to lock in each pick. The app records your session and generates a personalized settlement summary when the theme deck runs out.

Core Features

  • Head-tilt controls - Hands-free left/right selection using front-camera motion tracking
  • Theme packs - Curated “would you rather” question banks with bundled offline content
  • Built-in recording - Captures your reactions and choices in a vertical video
  • Settlement summary - Personalized result card at the end of each challenge
  • Save & share - Export to your photo library when you choose to save
  • No account - Core gameplay works without sign-in

How to Play

  • Open the app and pick a theme from the home screen
  • Tap start recording and allow camera (and microphone, if you want audio) when prompted
  • Tilt your head left or right when two options appear—each tilt locks one choice and brings the next pair
  • When the deck is finished, review your settlement summary and save the video if you like

Permissions

  • Camera - Front camera preview and head-tilt detection; video stays on your device during play
  • Microphone - Optional audio in your challenge recording
  • Photos - Used only when you tap save to store a finished video

FAQ

  • Head tilt not working? Hold the phone at eye level, face the front camera, and tilt clearly left or right. Some devices may respond more slowly in low light.
  • Recording failed or was blocked? Screen recording requires system permission. Open Settings → Privacy & Security → Screen Recording and ensure the app is allowed, then try again.
  • Can I play offline? Yes. Theme content is bundled in the app; an internet connection is not required for core gameplay.
  • Where does my video go? Recordings are processed on your device. They are saved to Photos only when you confirm save on the settlement screen.

Known Issues

  • Head-tracking sensitivity may vary by device, iOS version, and lighting.
  • Simulator builds may use sample video instead of live screen recording.
中文（简体）

「二选一：大冒险」是一款聚会向挑战游戏。镜头前会出现两个选项，向左或向右歪头即可锁定选择； 应用会录制全程，并在题库用完后生成专属结果总结。

核心功能

  • 歪头操控 - 通过前置镜头动作追踪，双手解放完成左右选择
  • 主题包 - 精选二选一题库，内置内容支持离线游玩
  • 内置录屏 - 收录表情反应与每次选择，生成竖屏视频
  • 结果总结 - 挑战结束后展示个性化结算卡片
  • 保存分享 - 在结算页确认后保存到相册
  • 无需账号 - 核心玩法不强制登录注册

如何游玩

  • 打开应用，在首页选择一个主题
  • 点击开始录制，按提示允许相机（如需音频则允许麦克风）
  • 出现两个选项时向左或向右歪头——每次选择锁定一侧并补上新选项
  • 题库用完后查看结果总结，满意即可保存视频

权限说明

  • 相机 - 用于前置预览与歪头检测；游玩过程中画面保留在设备本地
  • 麦克风 - 用于录制挑战视频的可选音频
  • 相册 - 仅在您点击保存已完成视频时使用

常见问题

  • 歪头没反应？ 请将手机举至与眼睛平齐、正对前置镜头，并做清晰的左右歪头动作。光线较暗时部分机型可能不够灵敏。
  • 录屏失败或被拒绝？ 录屏需要系统权限。请前往「设置 → 隐私与安全性 → 屏幕录制」，确认已允许本应用，然后重试。
  • 可以离线玩吗？ 可以。主题内容已内置在应用中，核心玩法无需联网。
  • 视频保存在哪里？ 录制在设备本地处理，仅在结算页确认保存后才会写入相册。

已知问题

  • 歪头识别灵敏度可能因设备、系统版本与光线条件而异。
  • 模拟器环境可能使用示例视频代替真实录屏。
